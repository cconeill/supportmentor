SupportMentor: Nurturing Tech Talent from Nontraditional Backgrounds
At SupportMentor, we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to pursue a career in tech. Our mission is to provide mentorship and resources to individuals from nontraditional backgrounds so they can succeed in the tech industry.
⋮
Our Community
At SupportMentor, we focus on nontraditional tech talent because we believe that diversity drives innovation. Our community is made up of individuals from diverse backgrounds who have the potential to make a difference in the tech industry.
⋯
Women in Tech
Our mentorship program provides women with the opportunity to gain the skills and experience they need to succeed in tech.
⋯
People of Color
We provide mentorship and resources to people of color to help them overcome obstacles and succeed in the tech industry.
⋯
LGBTQ+ Community
Our mentorship program is open and inclusive to everyone, including members of the LGBTQ+ community who are seeking to break into tech.
⋮
⋮
Our Approach to Mentorship
At SupportMentor, we provide one-on-one mentorship to help individuals from nontraditional backgrounds gain the skills and experience they need to succeed in tech. Our mentors are experienced tech professionals who are passionate about giving back to their communities.
Customized Learning
Our mentorship program is tailored to the needs of each individual, so you can learn at your own pace and focus on the skills that matter most to you.
⋯
Hands-On Experience
We provide opportunities for hands-on experience through internships and real-world projects to help you build your portfolio and gain practical experience.
⋯
Long-Term Support
Our mentorship doesn't end after a few months. We provide ongoing support and resources to help you succeed in your journey through the tech industry.
⋯
